Privacy & Data

What we collect and how it's used.

Information we collect: your name, email address, username, and PIN (used only to verify your identity when logging in), along with your assigned role/location and form permissions.

Information you submit: form entries you create in the app — pop-in reports, driver checklists, shortage reports, maintenance requests, damage reports, and (for sales staff) customer quotes — including any photos you attach.

Where it's stored: account and form data is stored in our Supabase database. Photos and generated PDF reports are stored in our company Dropbox and Google Drive. PDF generation is handled by a Google Apps Script we control.

How it's used: solely for internal Caliche's Frozen Custard business operations — staff login, task tracking, reporting, maintenance scheduling, notifications, and customer quotes. It is not sold or shared with outside parties beyond the service providers above that are necessary to run the app.

Your PIN: is stored so you can log in and is never shown to other staff. Admins can reset it but cannot view it.

What’s New — How to Use It

The new features built from Aaron’s feedback · June 2026

Everything below is live in the app right now. Tap a section heading in the menu to find it. Here’s what each one does and exactly how to use it.

⭐ Newest in this update

👤 Employee Profile

Where: Admin → Employee Roster → tap Profile on anyone (managers).

  1. See one complete record — role, store, status, certifications, cleared positions, and an Employment History of role changes.
  2. Tap ⬆ Change role to promote someone. It’s not one tap — you pick the new role, an effective date, and a reason, and it’s saved to their history and the audit log.
  3. A staff member can open their own profile, but not anyone else’s. Corrective actions (PIP) now live inside the profile, visible to admins only.

🚚 Supply Request

Where: the Supply Request tile on the menu (this replaced “Report Shortages”).

  1. New request: pick your store, urgency, and when you’ll run out, add a reason, then add each item (start typing — the catalog suggests names). Submit gives you a tracking number (e.g. SR-20260625-0001).
  2. My requests: watch the status of anything you sent.
  3. Managers/office: the Incoming tab is the queue. Tap a request to open it, see the status timeline, set the fulfillment source/transporter, and move it along (Assigned → Fulfilling → In transit → Received → Closed).
  4. Catalog tab (managers): add or edit the standard item list so names stay clean.

🤝 Crew Trainer

Where: the Crew Trainer tile (Crew Trainers, shift leads & managers).

  1. Managers: on the Assign & manage tab, pair a trainee with a trainer on a topic or station.
  2. The trainer: on My Trainees, tap Record coaching, pick the topic, and note what you covered (“guided them through supervised practice…”).
  3. This logs that coaching was delivered — a training note, kept separate from a competency certification.

🎓 Learning Paths (sample)

Where: Training & Resources → Learning Paths (everyone).

  1. Pick a path (White Apron, Register Certification…), then a course.
  2. Read each lesson page with Next / Back, then take the quick check — 80% to pass.
  3. Pass and it moves you to the next course automatically; finish them all to earn the path’s certification. Progress & badges show on the My Training screen.
  4. This is a sample to walk the flow — the wording is placeholder. Tap Reset progress to run it again.

🔒 Stay Logged In

Where: the login screen.

  1. There’s a “Keep me logged in on this device” checkbox — leave it on and the app won’t make you re-enter your PIN every time it refreshes or auto-updates.
  2. Fully close and reopen the app and it asks for your PIN again (so it stays secure on shared tablets).
  3. Logout clears it on that device.
Earlier updates

🎉 Recognition & Shout-Outs

Where: Home screen (the recognition card).

  1. Tap “Give a Shout-Out.”
  2. Pick a person (or a team/whole store), choose the type (shout-out, customer compliment, great work…), and write your message.
  3. Submit. A manager approves it, then it appears on everyone’s Home feed and the person gets a notification.
  4. Managers: tap the approvals button to approve or decline pending ones.

✅ Corrective Actions on Inspections

Where: Work → Store Pop-In Inspection.

  1. Do the Pop-In as usual and submit.
  2. Any item you scored 1 or 2 is collected into a Corrective Actions popup.
  3. For each one, pick who fixes it, a due date, and whether a photo is required. Uncheck any item to skip it.
  4. Save — each becomes a task automatically assigned to that person.

🚨 Emergency Mode

Where: Red “Emergency Procedures” button on the Home screen.

  1. Tap a scenario (power outage, fire, robbery/threat, machine failure, water, food safety…).
  2. Follow the short step-by-step checklist. It works even with no internet.
  3. Still to do: management fills in the real phone numbers where you see [____].

📚 Training & Resources

Where: “Training & Resources” shortcut on Home, or the All-Sections menu.

  1. Everyone can open it and read modules grouped by category (Recipes, How-To, etc.).
  2. Admins: tap “+ Add module,” or Edit/Remove on any module, to keep recipe books and lessons up to date.
  3. Shortcut buttons jump to Mr. Scoopy (Q&A) and the Forms library.

⚙️ Equipment History + QR Codes

Where: Work → Maintenance & Damage → Equipment History.

  1. Pick the store and search for a machine, then tap it to see the full record — model, serial, vendor, warranty, manuals, troubleshooting, repair history and current status.
  2. Tap “Print QR label” for one machine, or “Print all QR codes” for the whole store, and stick them on the machines. Scanning a code opens that machine’s record.
  3. Admins: “+ Add equipment” creates a new record and its QR automatically; Edit/Remove are on each record.

🔧 Maintenance → Equipment Link

Where: Work → Report a Repair.

  1. The form now has an Equipment dropdown — pick the machine from the registry instead of typing it (or choose “Other”).
  2. From any equipment record, the “Report maintenance” button opens the form with that machine already selected.

📝 Pre-Shift Lineup — now position-first

Where: Schedule → Pre-Shift Lineup (shift leads & managers).

  1. Pick the store, date, and AM or PM (each is its own saved lineup), and type your 2 shift goals.
  2. The lineup now lists the positions — Shift Leader, Machine, two Registers, Item Maker. In each, type a name or pick it from the list (it suggests your crew as you type; you can even type a name that isn’t in the system yet).
  3. Bigger shift? Tap ➕ Add slot for more spots, or the × to remove one. Then Save.
  4. Tap the 📈 icon on a slot to see that person’s training progression. A reminder still goes out twice a day to set the lineup.

🛒 Pick n’ Take Orders (via Cherry)

Where: In Cherry (the assistant), Ask Cherry.

  1. Forward or paste a Pick n’ Take order email into Ask Cherry.
  2. Review what she’ll do and approve.
  3. She creates two tasks on that store’s board — a 🍦 PREP task and a 🛒 PICKUP task, both dated to the pickup.

🔧 Preventive Maintenance

Where: Work → Equipment History → open any machine.

  1. Scroll to 🔧 Preventive maintenance on the machine’s record. Managers tap ➕ Add to set a recurring service (e.g. “Sanitize & clean every 7 days”).
  2. When it’s done, anyone on shift taps ✅ Mark serviced — it logs who & when and rolls the next-due date forward.
  3. Overdue or due-soon items show a red/amber badge, and the 🔧 Maintenance due button at the top of Equipment History lists everything due for that store.
  4. Managers also get a daily push each morning when machines are due.

📞 Call a Coworker from the Roster

Where: Admin → Employee Roster (managers).

  1. Open the roster and find the person — if they have a phone number on file, a green 📞 Call button shows on their row.
  2. Tap it to phone them straight from the app — handy when someone hasn’t shown up for their shift.
  3. To add or change a number, tap Edit on their card and fill in the new Phone field.

⚙️ Equipment: Warehouse + Maintenance History

Where: Work → Equipment History.

  1. The store dropdown now includes Warehouse — it lists warehouse equipment split into ✅ In use and 📦 Unused backups. Managers tap In use / Backup on a unit to move it between the two.
  2. Open any machine to see its new Maintenance reports history — every request filed for it, with status.
  3. Managers can tap ✅ Mark resolved on a report and type how it was fixed; the resolution then shows in that machine’s history.
  4. All your real equipment (walk-in coolers, freezers, washers, dryers, water heaters & softeners, Lenox’s two custard machines) is now loaded per store.

✅ Confirm Your Schedule

Where: Schedule → My Shifts.

  1. Once you have checked your shifts for the week, tap ✅ Confirm my schedule at the bottom.
  2. It lets your manager know you have seen the week.
  3. Managers: in the schedule’s left tools, open ✅ Confirmations to see who has (and has not) confirmed each week.
